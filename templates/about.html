  1. discreetly cautious
    1. hesitant and vigilant about dangers and risks
    2. slow to grant, accept, or expend

App Summary

Chary is a tool to help users become more cautious and thoughtful with how they spend their money. Chary makes budgeting easy. Users can keep track of their expenses, set spending goals, and visualise how well they do over time. And because 'chary' sounds like 'cherry', we embraced the pun and made Chary to be cherry-themed!

Development Details

This app was created during the summer of 2022 as a passion project. It uses Python and the framework Flask for the backend. Regular HTML, CSS, and Javascript was used for the frontend. This app uses Google Cloud's firestore to store data and is hosted using Google App Engine. This app also takes advantage of Google's OAuth for signing users in.
